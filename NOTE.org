- To init HAProxy log:
  1. Add ~log globla~ or ~log ...~ to ~frontend~ section in [/etc/haproxy/haproxy.cfg].
  2. Add instructions below to [/etc/rsyslog.d/49-haproxy.conf].
#+BEGIN_SRC
$ModLoad imudp
$UDPServerAddress *
$UDPServerRun 514
#+END_SRC
